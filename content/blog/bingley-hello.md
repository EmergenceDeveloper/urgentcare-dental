Bingley sits in one of those valleys that makes you understand why people settled in Yorkshire in the first place.

The Aire running through. The Five Rise Locks stepping up the hillside like something from another century, because they are. The moors visible from town on a clear day. It's the kind of place where you can be in proper countryside within ten minutes of your front door, but still catch a train into Leeds or Bradford when you need to.

We're UrgentCare Dental, based over in Leeds at Whitehall Estate in Armley. That's about twenty-five minutes from Bingley by car, or you can come through Shipley if you prefer. Not exactly next door, but close enough that we wanted to say hello.
