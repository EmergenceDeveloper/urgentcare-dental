There's something about Pudsey that resists being swallowed up by Leeds.

It's technically part of the city now, has been since the 1974 boundary changes, but it never quite feels that way. The town centre still has its own gravity. The park, the market, Pudsey St Lawrence cricket club in the summer. People say they're *from* Pudsey, not from Leeds. That distinction matters.

We're at UrgentCare Dental, based in Armley at Whitehall Estate. Depending on which part of Pudsey you're in, that's somewhere between five and fifteen minutes away. Closer than the city centre, probably closer than wherever you've been going.

We wanted to introduce ourselves properly.

## The Invitation

If you've been meaning to sort your teeth out but haven't got around to it, consider this a nudge.

We're not going to ask why it's been a while. We're not going to make you feel bad about anything. We're just going to help you get to where you want to be, at prices that are [clear and upfront](/pricing).

Pudsey's a good place. Unpretentious, solid, gets on with things. We like to think we're a bit the same.

If you ever need a dentist, we're just down the road.
