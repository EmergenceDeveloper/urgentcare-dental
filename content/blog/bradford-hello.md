Bradford has a presence that's hard to miss.

It's a city that's been through a lot and wears its history openly. The wool exchange, the Alhambra, Cartwright Hall up in Lister Park. Little Germany with its merchant buildings still standing. There's a weight to the place, a sense that things have happened here and will keep happening.

It's also right next door to us.

UrgentCare Dental is in Leeds, at Whitehall Estate in Armley. From most of Bradford, that's fifteen to twenty minutes. From the city centre, you're looking at a straight shot down the A647. We see patients from Bradford regularly, and we wanted to make this a bit more official.

Hello, Bradford. We're glad you're nearby.
